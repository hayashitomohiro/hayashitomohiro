Photography for the poetry collection 違和感しかない世界で

Poem & Recitation Waka

Poems: Poem & Recitation Waka

Photographs: HAYASHI Tomohiro

Design: MOTOMURA Tatsuhiko

I contributed the photography for 違和感しかない世界で(In a World Filled with Nothing but Discomfort), a poetry collection by Waka, a poet and spoken-word performer. Working closely with Waka and the designer, Motomura, we developed the book through a series of discussions. It features a total of 24 photographs selected to accompany Waka’s words, ranging from images I had taken over the years to new photographs created specifically for the book.

Price: ¥1,000-
To purchase a copy, please visit Waka’s online shop.
