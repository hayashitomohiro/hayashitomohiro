照山紅葉｜TERUYAMA Momiji

From 2021 until around March 2025, I released music and photography under the name TERUYAMA Momiji. In 2025, after joining a research lab at university and beginning academic-oriented work, I started to reconsider what had until then been a personal, hobbyist practice. Because of this, I am currently refraining from publicly releasing new compositions in the way I used to (though I do still have a few pieces in progress).

At the same time, I have been moving away from thinking in terms of academic versus non-academic, and returning to work rooted in my own interests and curiosity. Going forward, I would like to continue making music as one form of my own expression, at a comfortable pace. Along the way, I would also be glad for opportunities to create something together with others.
