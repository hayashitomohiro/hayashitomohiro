林朋紘 HAYASHI Tomohiro

Born in Tokyo in 2003, raised in Toyama, Japan.

Currently in the first year of the Master's program in Acoustic Design at the Graduate School of Design, Kyushu University.

Alongside playing instruments and composing music, I am interested in recording media such as photography and audio recording, and create works and performances that explore how such records are perceived by people.

I am also part of a three-person unit called 〈Sahanji〉, together with photographer MATSUDA Momoka and graphic designer YAMASAKI Suzumitsu, working across our respective fields of photography, design, and sound to plan and create projects.
