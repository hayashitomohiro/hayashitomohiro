Film: Bookaholic Circle

Music／TERUYAMA Momiji

　I composed the original score for Bookaholic Circle, an independent film set in Kiyama, Miyaki District, Saga Prefecture, Japan. Following the story of a peculiar book club where avid readers with hidden pasts gather and where love and delusion intertwine, I created a melancholic solo guitar theme as well as additional score pieces to accompany scene transitions throughout the film.
