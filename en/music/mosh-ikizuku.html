Ikizuku

Music: TERUYAMA Momiji

Artwork: mosh, TERUYAMA Momiji

　I created the venue music for the second half of Ikizuku (Breathing), a solo exhibition by mosh held at Kuwanomi Cafe from March 17 to 23, 2025. This recording features an improvised performance based on poems written for the exhibition, performed as the artist installed the works. The performance was created using a sampler loaded with recordings of a piano originally recorded at my family home and repeatedly played back and re-recorded in various locations.
